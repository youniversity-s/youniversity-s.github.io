The Ateneo de Manila University is a private Jesuit research university in Quezon City, Philippines. It was founded in 1859 by the Society of Jesus as the Escuela Municipal de Manila, a publicly-funded primary school in Intramuros, Manila, making it one of the oldest Jesuit-administered institutions of higher learning in the Asia-Pacific. The patron of the university is Saint Ignatius of Loyola
