De La Salle University

De La Salle University has established itself as a leader in the development of human resources for the church and the nation. The Brothers of the Christian Schools founded it in 1911 as a Catholic coeducational college. Academic excellence, prolific and relevant research, and active community involvement distinguish the University as a hub for higher education training.

Religio, Mores, Cultura

Established in 1911, De La Salle University is a non-profit private higher-education institution located in the urban setting of the metropolis of Manila, National Capital Region. This institution also has branch campuses in the following locations: Laguna, Bonifacio Global City, Makati. Officially recognized by the Commission on Higher Education of the Philippines, De La Salle University (DLSU) is a large coeducational Philippine higher education institution formally affiliated with the Christian-Catholic religion.
