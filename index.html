MISSION AND VISION

This website aims to help incoming college freshmen in choosing the optimal universities based on their preferences and circumstances. We can serve as their guide to the foremost and most desired universities from Metro Manila.

To establish YOUniversity as a prime guide to help students be more decisive about their paths with a wider and more inclusive range of universities.
